Start with the highlighted execution loop to follow the work. Then use the control plane to see who decides, intervention granularity to locate where it acts, and the substrate to see what supports it. Papers may appear in multiple parts, so counts are not additive. Lists are newest first.

Structural guarantee

Exact verification and runtime reuse are the clearest wins: draft–verify / Faster Cascades and serving reuse (M021, M109–M112, M223), with the judge-reuse exception M226.

Informative signal

Failure zone
