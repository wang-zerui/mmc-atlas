Definition · what counts

Collaboration requires composition, not merely plurality

Two or more generative model instances contribute to one task outcome through an explicit interaction protocol.

instances + topology + exchange → outcome

Included

Distinct checkpoints, role-differentiated calls on one checkpoint, or replicated samples whose outputs are explicitly composed.

Seven axes

Heterogeneity · topology · coupling · awareness · interaction unit · exchanged state · objective.

Excluded

Pure RAG, non-generative tools, human-only collaboration, and within-checkpoint MoE routing.

Orchestration & debate · the negative evidence

More agents are not automatically more intelligence

−41.1%teams vs their own best identified expert
2.1–3.4×debate tokens for equal or worse accuracy than isolated self-correction
29%strict conformity flips — mostly harmful
2 ≈ 16diverse agents can match many homogeneous agents

When orchestration helps

  • Roles or capabilities are genuinely heterogeneous
  • Topology adapts to task structure
  • Workers return verifiable, scoped artifacts
  • A controller can stop or reject bad collaboration

When it backfires

  • Homogeneous peers converge on the same error
  • Consensus dilutes the strongest expert
  • Extra context and calls masquerade as mechanism gains
  • No signal says whose answer should dominate

Verification · the strongest quality lever

A verifier helps only if its own validity is measured

Positive

Process supervision, compute-optimal search, disjoint-family panels, and environment-aware verifiers can materially improve selection.

Caveat

On ~541K judgments, exact-match agreement inflated quality by 33–41 percentage points versus chance-corrected κ.

Systems interaction

KV reuse that helps generators can weaken cross-candidate attention and silently break judges.

Takeaways

Five conclusions from the evidence

1 · Collaboration is a control problem

Route, orchestrate, verify, and stop — around an execution loop with shared state.

2 · More calls are not a mechanism

Matched-compute evidence often removes the apparent gain from teams, debate, and voting.

3 · Guarantees and signals are the durable levers

Exact verification, reliable uncertainty, honest diversity, and workflow-aware reuse.

4 · Agentic work changes the unit of optimization

The target becomes a stateful trajectory, workflow, or program — not one static answer.

5 · Read the paper-level evidence

The Atlas exposes every family, leaf topic, TL;DR, result, limitation, and source.
