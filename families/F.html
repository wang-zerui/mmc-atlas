F. Strong-Weak Efficiency

44 papers
Weak model does bulk work; strong model verifies or refines.

Base. This family contains both formally lossless speculative decoding and lossy semantic context reduction. Speculative decoding (M005/M006) preserves the target distribution; EAGLE (M007), SpecInfer (M081), Staged (M082), and heterogeneous-vocabulary TLI (M086) extend it. LLMLingua (M089/M090), AgentDiet (M092), and ACON (M093) instead compress prompts or agent trajectories.

Trajectory compression, 2025–26. A strict screen — later model calls consume reduced state derived from prior observations/actions/tool results — now yields five mechanism groups: filtering (M323/M325/M333), learned running state (M322/M324/M330/M331/M340), proactive folding (M327/M328/M334/M338), recoverable external evidence (M326/M336), and serving/lookahead control (M337/M339). RE-TRAC (M335, ICML'26) writes typed evidence, uncertainty, failures, and plans across trajectories. Context-Folding and RE-TRAC are ICML'26; MEM1, AgentFold, IterResearch, and domain-adaptive LightMem M340 are ICLR'26. The different 2025 LightMem M329 was withdrawn.

Do not call this lossless. Semantic summaries and pruning almost always discard information. PAL-UI, U-Fold, and MemexRL are recoverable because raw evidence remains external, but retrieval can still fail. Metrics also differ — active context, cumulative tokens, FLOPs, turns, wall time, and success are not interchangeable. ReSum was desk-rejected, R_FOLD is unaccepted, and Focus M332 reports only N=5. By contrast, 2026 speculative-decoding work such as Saguaro (M190) and BASTION (M192) retains a structural distribution-preservation guarantee; VSD/PARSE/TreeFlash remain approximate tradeoffs.

M190
Variational Speculative Decoding / VSD (Zou et al. 2026)
2026: retrains the drafter via variational inference to maximize sequence-level target-model ACCEPTANCE (ELBO + EM) rather than token likelihood; distribution preservation not claimed (approximate, likely lossy on the drafter objective).
M191
BASTION (Oh et al. 2026)
2026: budget-aware speculative decoding with tree-structured BLOCK-DIFFUSION drafting, adaptively growing per-query draft trees via an acceptance surrogate + hardware roofline latency estimator; training-free and LOSSLESS.
M192
PARSE: Parallel Prefix Verification (Yao et al. 2026)
2026: verifies multiple draft PREFIXES semantically in one forward pass (custom attention mask) to find the longest valid prefix; orthogonal to and composable with token-level spec-decode; near-lossless (negligible accuracy loss, so technically LOSSY).
M193
Cross-Family Speculative Decoding via UAG (Fonal 2026)
2026: empirical study of cross-FAMILY / cross-tokenizer speculative decoding (Mistral-based Bielik-11B target with Qwen/Llama drafters) via Universal Assisted Generation token translation on Apple Silicon; content-dependent gains, losslessness not established under token translation.
M194

2026: adds a lightweight MLP conditioned on drafter hidden-state + previous token to APPROXIMATE the autoregressive distribution for one-shot block/tree drafters at O(1) decode cost, correcting the marginal-distribution drift of parallel drafting; approximate (not proven lossless).
M195
