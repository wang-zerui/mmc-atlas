F. Strong-Weak Efficiency

25 papers
Weak model does bulk work; strong model verifies or refines.

Base. The one family with formal guarantees. Speculative decoding
(M005/M006) is exact-distribution 2–3×; EAGLE (M007) 2.7–6.5×; a large lossless
family (SpecInfer M081, Staged M082, Self-Speculative M083, Lookahead M080, CS
Drafting M087, REST M085, heterogeneous-vocab TLI M086); lossy siblings are
flagged as such (Medusa-2 M079, BiLD M088). Prompt/context compression (LLMLingua
M089/M090) and distillation (Orca M091) are strong→weak transfers, and agentic
context pruning (AgentDiet M092, ACON M093) extends this to trajectories.
2026 SOTA. The 2026 crop sharpens the lossless/lossy line the survey insists
on: lossless — Saguaro (M190, ICLR'26) breaks the draft/verify dependency by
pre-speculating verification (~30% over spec-decode, up to 5×); BASTION (M192)
budget-aware tree block-diffusion drafting, training-free, up to 6.61×.
Lossy / approximate — VSD (M191) retrains the drafter to maximize acceptance
(no distribution guarantee); PARSE (M193) semantic prefix verification
("negligible" but nonzero degradation); TreeFlash (M195) approximates the AR
distribution; cross-family UAG (M194) establishes no losslessness under token
translation; ConfLayers (M196) is framed as a speed–quality tradeoff. The takeaway
is unchanged and reinforced: coupling delivers robust gains because the
verification step carries a structural (distribution-preserving) guarantee.

Papers in this family

Speculative Decoding (Leviathan et al. 2023)
A small drafter proposes tokens that the large target model verifies in parallel, giving exact-distribution speedups without retraining or architecture changes.
M005ICML 2023 (Oral)
Speculative Sampling (Chen et al. 2023, DeepMind)
A concurrent draft model proposes short continuations that the target scores in parallel, with a modified rejection-sampling step accelerating decoding losslessly.
M006arXiv 2023 (DeepMind)
EAGLE (Li et al. 2024)
EAGLE drafts at the second-to-top-layer feature level (not token level) and resolves feature uncertainty by conditioning on a one-step-shifted token sequence, raising acceptance and speedup.
M007ICML 2024
Big Little Decoder (Kim et al. 2023)
A small model decodes autoregressively while a large model is called occasionally to refine only uncertain predictions non-autoregressively, via learned fallback and rollback policies.
M008NeurIPS 2023
Medusa (Cai et al. 2024)
Extra lightweight decoding heads on a frozen backbone predict several future tokens in parallel, verified via tree attention; Medusa-1 is lossless while Medusa-2 uses typical-acceptance.
M079ICML 2024
Lookahead Decoding (Fu et al. 2024)
A draft-model-free, exact parallel decoding algorithm that uses Jacobi iteration and n-gram trajectories to break the sequential dependency of autoregressive decoding.
M080ICML 2024
SpecInfer (Miao et al. 2023)
Small speculative models propose a token tree that the LLM verifies in parallel as a tree verifier, provably preserving output quality while cutting latency.
M081ASPLOS 2024
Staged Speculative Decoding (Spector & Re 2023)
Restructures the speculative batch as a tree and adds a second speculation stage (speculating on the draft itself) to accelerate small-batch, on-device inference losslessly.
M082ICML 2023 ES-FOMO Workshop
Self-Speculative Decoding / Draft & Verify (Zhang et al. 2023)
The model drafts by selectively skipping its own intermediate layers, then verifies those tokens with the full model in one forward pass, making it draft-model-free and lossless.
M083ACL 2024
Blockwise Parallel Decoding (Stern et al. 2018)
The foundational draft-verify idea: predict k future steps in parallel with auxiliary heads, then back off to the longest prefix a scoring model validates.
M084NeurIPS 2018
REST: Retrieval-Based Speculative Decoding (He et al. 2023)
Draft tokens are retrieved from a datastore of common text patterns instead of generated by a draft model, making acceleration plug-and-play with no training.
M085NAACL 2024
Lossless SD for Heterogeneous Vocabularies / TLI (Timor et al. 2025)
Three new speculative decoding algorithms (including Token-Level Intersection) remove the shared-vocabulary constraint so any off-the-shelf model can draft for any target, losslessly.
M086ICML 2025 (Oral)
Cascade Speculative Drafting / CS Drafting (Chen et al. 2023)
A vertical cascade replaces slow autoregressive drafting with tiers of smaller models, and a horizontal cascade optimizes drafting-time allocation across tokens, while preserving the target distribution.
M087NeurIPS 2024
LLMLingua (Jiang et al. 2023)
A small aligned LM compresses prompts coarse-to-fine before the large model reads them, delegating context reduction to a weak model to cut cost.
M089EMNLP 2023
LLMLingua-2 (Pan et al. 2024)
Distills GPT-4's compression knowledge into a small bidirectional encoder that does prompt compression as token classification, faster and more faithful than LLMLingua.
M090ACL 2024 (Findings)
Orca (Mukherjee et al. 2023)
Distillation as strong-weak transfer: a 13B model imitates GPT-4's step-by-step explanation traces rather than just output style, a training-time coupling that later runs solo.
M091arXiv 2023 (Microsoft Research)
AgentDiet (Xiao et al. 2025)
An inference-time trajectory-reduction method that automatically prunes redundant, useless, and expired information from a software agent's growing context to cut input-token cost without hurting performance.
M092FSE 2026
ACON: Agent Context Optimization (Kang et al. 2025)
Optimizes natural-language compression guidelines from failure analysis, then distills the LLM compressor into a small model so a weak model condenses context for a strong long-horizon agent.
M093ICML 2026
Speculative Speculative Decoding / Saguaro (Kumar, Dao & May 2026)
Parallelizes the draft/verify dependency by pre-speculating likely verification outcomes during verification (a CPU speculative-execution analogy), eliminating drafting overhead; lossless.
M190ICLR 2026
Variational Speculative Decoding / VSD (Zou et al. 2026)
Retrains the drafter via variational inference to maximize sequence-level target-model acceptance (ELBO + EM) rather than token likelihood, targeting the real multi-path decoding objective.
M191arXiv 2026
BASTION (Oh et al. 2026)
Budget-aware speculative decoding with tree-structured block-diffusion drafting that adaptively grows per-query draft trees using an acceptance surrogate and a hardware roofline latency estimator; training-free and lossless.
M192arXiv 2026
PARSE: Parallel Prefix Verification (Yao et al. 2026)
Verifies multiple draft prefixes semantically in a single forward pass via a custom attention mask to find the longest valid prefix, orthogonal to and composable with token-level speculative decoding.
M193arXiv 2026
Cross-Family Speculative Decoding via UAG (Fonal 2026)
An empirical study of cross-family, cross-tokenizer speculative decoding (Mistral-based Bielik-11B target with Qwen/Llama drafters) via Universal Assisted Generation token translation on Apple Silicon.
M194arXiv 2026
TreeFlash (Rheinboldt et al. 2026)
Adds a lightweight MLP conditioned on the drafter's hidden state and previous token to approximate the autoregressive distribution for one-shot block/tree drafters at O(1) decode cost, correcting parallel-drafting drift.
M195arXiv 2026
ConfLayers (Amer et al. 2026)
Training-free self-speculative decoding that forms the drafter by confidence-based intermediate-layer skipping with an adaptive threshold, with the full target still verifying, so the mechanism is intended distribution-preserving.
M196arXiv 2026
